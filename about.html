Hi, I'm Demitra
but you can call me Demi

Based in Athens, Greece

A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one. With an intense love for Mathematics and Design, I studied Architecture, where in the stressful times of presentations, I found solace in the world of Origami. While searching in Japanese books I couldn't understand for the next origami design to create, I promised myself that I will learn this language one day. In the meantime, I worked as a content creator, copywriter, social media Manager, Customer Experience specialist, learned Calligraphy, created my own origami brand, and maintained this unquenched thirst of learning whatever comes my way. This brings us to today, where I am discovering the world of Front-end Development, while learning Japanese, working on my origami and trying to (unsuccessfully may I add) get through my ever-growing TBR pile of books.
